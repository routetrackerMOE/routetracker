About Us

Get to know a bit about us, The creaters!!

The Creators

A group of passionate students driven by creativity and innovation. Together, we bring a diverse set of skills and perspectives to the table, united by their shared love for learning and creating.

Mission

To ensure safe, reliable, and efficient transportation for children commuting to school by providing up-to-date and easy-to-access bus route information.

Vision

We aim to break new ground through creative thinking and problem-solving, while shaping a future where students are empowered to transform their ideas into meaningful, real-world solutions.
